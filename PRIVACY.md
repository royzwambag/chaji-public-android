# Privacy Policy for Cha Ji (Android)

**Last updated: 27 May 2026**

Cha Ji is a personal tea brewing journal. This policy explains what happens to your information when
you use the app. The short version: Cha Ji does not collect your personal data, does not track you,
and contains no ads or analytics.

## Who we are

Cha Ji is developed by Roy Zwambag. If you have any questions about this policy, create an issue in
this repo.

## Data you create in the app

Cha Ji lets you record teas and brewing sessions, including names, types, origins, vendors, ratings,
notes, brewing parameters, and an optional link to where you bought a tea. This content is created
and stored by you.

This data is saved locally on your device, in the app's private storage. The developer has no access
to it.

## Backups

Cha Ji has no account, no login, and no developer-run server — your journal never reaches us. There
are two ways your data can leave the device, both under your control:

- **Android Auto Backup.** If device backup is enabled, Android may automatically back up the app's
  database and settings to your own Google Drive, so your journal can be restored when you reinstall
  the app or set up a new device. This backup lives in your personal Google account; it is not sent
  to the developer and the developer cannot read it. Google's handling of this data is governed by
  Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
- **Manual export / import.** You can export your journal to a JSON file and import it later. These
  files are written to the location you choose; the app never uploads them anywhere.

If device backup is turned off and you don't export anything, your data simply stays on your device.

## App settings

Your preferences — such as default tea type, label style, temperature unit, sort order, and
appearance — are stored locally on your device using the system preferences store (DataStore). They
may be included in Android Auto Backup as described above; otherwise they never leave your device.

## What we do not do

- We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
- We do not use third-party SDKs that collect your data, and we do not share your data with third
  parties.
- We do not require an account or a login.

## Links to other sites

If you save a purchase or vendor link to a tea and then tap it, the link opens in your browser. Any
website you visit that way is outside Cha Ji and has its own privacy policy.

## Children

Cha Ji does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

## Deleting your data

You can delete any tea or brewing session inside the app. Uninstalling the app deletes the data
stored on your device. A copy kept by Android Auto Backup can be managed or removed through your
device's backup settings in your Google account.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new
"Last updated" date.
